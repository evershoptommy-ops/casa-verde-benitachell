About the home

A bright duplex retreat above the Costa Blanca

Set on the sunny hillside of Benitachell, between Moraira and Jávea, Casa Verde is a freshly renovated duplex with sweeping sea and mountain views. Mornings start with coffee on the balcony; afternoons drift by at the pool just steps from the door.

Two bright bedrooms, a rooftop terrace made for sunsets, and a pool open year-round — with the cove at Cala del Moraig only minutes away. It's the kind of place you settle into and never quite want to leave.

— Your hosts at Casa Verde
